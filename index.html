    Welcome to McqMatrix

    McqMatrix is a leading online platform dedicated to providing high-quality, comprehensive Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) for competitive exam preparation.

    Our mission is to empower students and professionals aiming for PPSC, SPSC, FPSC, IBA, NTS, and CSS exams with the best free resources available on the internet.

    Privacy Policy

    How we handle your data

    1. Information Collection

    McqMatrix does not generally collect personally identifiable information unless you explicitly provide it (e.g., creating an admin account). We may use local storage to save your quiz progress and settings locally on your device.

    2. Cookies & Advertising

    We use third-party advertising companies, such as Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our Website. These companies may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website or other websites.

    3. Firebase Analytics

    We utilize Google Firebase to aggregate anonymous usage data to improve application performance and user experience.
